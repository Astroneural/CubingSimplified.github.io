All 21 PLL Algorithms

Permuting Last Layer, or PLL, is the final step in the CFOP method, also known as the Fridrich method. One-look and two-look are the two main PLL set methods. The first method permutes the entire last layer. The second method is similar to the first, but with a few more steps. After the OLL step (Orienting Last Layer), this step follows. There are precisely 21 PLL cases, each labeled with a letter in the English alphabet. The algorithm name, an image of the case, and available algorithms to solve the case are given below, as is information about the case including the category, alternate names, algorithm sets the algorithm is used in, and probability of the case appearing in a solve.
